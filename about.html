About This Site

Built by and for the Auburn High School Class of 2028.

Instagram @ahs.class.of28 Contact Officers

Why it exists

Phones aren’t allowed during school. This site gives students a fast way to:

  • See what fundraisers are live right now
  • Check the event calendar & no-school days
  • Submit media for highlights
  • Contact class officers quickly

What’s here

  • Fundraisers: Live/Upcoming/Past with times
  • Calendar: Today highlighted + next/previous event
  • Media: Photos & video grid; student submissions
  • Achievements: Wall of recognition
  • Merch: Class gear (coming soon)
  • Admin: Quick edits to data (officers only)

How it’s built

Fast static pages with a glass UI and starfield background. Fundraisers and events load from simple JSON so dates/times are easy to update on GitHub—no coding needed.

Media & Submissions

Students can submit photos/videos for the Media page. Submissions are reviewed by officers before posting.

  • School-appropriate content only
  • Get permission from people in the photo/video
  • No personal phone numbers or private info
